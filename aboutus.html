About Us

The purpose of designing a website like this is to provide a service where people can be up to date on any litter problems relating to their respective borough and providing an easy way to report any litter problems they see within their community.

Features

Live camera feed for parks in the boroughs.
Able to report anonymously of people who litter in the contact us page.
Have statistics on the amount of litter on the streets in each borough.
